VALUES

OUR VALUES

Everything we do starts with our values – this is our promise of how we are going to work together and treat our partners.

ETHICAL AND PRINCIPLED

Working with Hidden Harbor, the experience was better than my expectations. I had heard the goods and bads of working for private equity owned companies, I mean they were absolute professionals at what they do. They were a great soundboard for me, and a great mentor for me and my team.

ONE TEAM

All for one, one for all. We win and lose as one team. Our collaborative working style emphasizes teamwork, trust and tolerance for diverging opinions. We genuinely care for and support not only those with whom we work shoulder-to-shoulder, but also our investors, partner companies and the customers and communities we serve.

FOCUS ON IMPROVING

All firms make mistakes. Rather than pretending we are perfect, we strive to identify our mistakes and learn from them. Mistakes that have been acknowledged and fixed should be celebrated and not denied.

TENACIOUS ABOUT WINNING

We are enthusiastic and dedicated to outperforming our peers in all that we do and have a burning desire to get beyond adequate to exceptional. We roll up our sleeves, dig in, and come to work every day searching for better outcomes. We relentlessly tackle tough problems. We see things through.

EMBRACE AND DRIVE CHANGE

We never accept or get too comfortable with the status quo because, historically, the firms that get into trouble are often the ones that are not able to respond quickly enough and adapt to change. We are continuously learning and ever evolving.

GREAT PARTNERS

We are committed to our partners and every day we treat their money as if it were our own. Great partnerships are built by focusing on the long term and doing what is right not just for ourselves but also for our partners.

HUMILITY

We believe success without humility is not success. We believe that no matter what happens we should always be respectful of everyone. While we celebrate our successes, we are not arrogant, nor do we treat others differently from how we would want to be treated. Instead, we carry ourselves with a quiet confidence because we believe that, in the long run, our character will speak for itself.

    CORPORATE SOCIAL RESPONSIBILITY

    Hidden Harbor is committed to building businesses that have a positive impact on their communities

    • We invest on behalf of institutional investors around the world, including retirement systems and university endowments, to make companies better positioned for long-term growth
    • As careful stewards of capital, we strive to provide solutions and support which create lasting value for our investors, portfolio companies, and society at large
    • We have helped scores of companies become stronger and more competitive, and in doing so, bolstered the communities in which their employees live and work by generating jobs and strengthening economies
    • Our commitment to operate with the utmost integrity and publicly minded spirit is a central element of the firm’s culture and reflected in the work we do every day

    INVESTING RESPONSIBLY

    Our commitment to ESG and corporate responsibility is embedded into our diligence processes and central to every investment decision we make.

    FOSTERING GROWTH

    Creating better and larger businesses of enduring value is a vital part of Hidden Harbor’s mission and is what motivates us most about our approach.

    IMPROVING SUSTAINABILITY

    Protecting the environment of the communities in which we operate is critically important and something we believe is important for the long-term.
